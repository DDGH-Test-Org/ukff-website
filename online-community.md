title: Social Media & Online Communities
---

UK Freeforms manages a number of online communities which you can join to get announcements of new events and games, to discuss topics related to freeforming, and ask any questions you have.

| Platform  | Name                        | Description                                                |
| --------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Discord   | [UK Freeforms Clubhouse][1] | Real time text chat with occassionally used voice channels |
| Groups.io | [UKFreeforms][2]            | A combined email list and web forum                        |
| Facebook  | [UK Freeforms][3]           | A group on Meta's primary social media service             |

Some of our events have their own online communities which members and interested people can join.
