Consequences

Consequences is an annual larp convention held at Chichester Lakeside Holiday Park in November. It runs from Thursday evening to Sunday afternoon, with typically more than 40 games offered to xxx attendees.

We also play a lot of boardgames and sometimes some tabletop roleplaying games.
