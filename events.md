## Retford Weekenders

The Retford Weekend game takes place in a hotel during early February and consists of a single game for between 65 and 100 players which begins on Friday evening and finishes early Sunday afternoon with breaks for food and rest.

## Consequences

Consequences is a convention at a holiday camp just outside Chichester during November hosting around 150 games typically lasting 3–4 hours between Thursday evening and late Sunday afternoon.

## Bristol Freeform Games Day

Bristol Freeform Gamers main event consists of a 2–4 games across two time slots on a Saturday in August. It is designed to be an affordable event with a goal to draw new players into our hobby.
